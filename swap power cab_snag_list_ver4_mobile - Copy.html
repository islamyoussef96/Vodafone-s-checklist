Swap Cabinet Checklist























Power Sf6 is not Matched
Does the site match with the Power SF6?
Does the Ac CB Rating and the cable size match with the Power sf6 ?
Dismantled Material
Are the Dismantle Materials Returned To WH?
Wrong alarm definition
Are Power cabinet alarms defined?
Are Enclosure cabinet alarms defined ?
Are You Check Environmental alarm (Door , temp , Flood and Smoke ) Connected to Power cabinet In case Of Shelter
Are the Sensors of PC1000 (humidity, Water,temp,smoke & door) exist and connected ?
Hybrid system is not Working
In case Of Hybrid system , Has Self Check done successfully and hybrid parameter checked?
In case Of Hybrid system ,Are Hybrid Cables and fuel sensor cable Connected and Secured?
Wrong power connectivity
Are all the Radio DCDUs Connected as Standard?
Are all the TX DCDUs' Connected as Standard?
Are TX Nodes connected to suitalble CB Or DCDU fuse and protected
Have Power Cables for batteries installed as standards
Does External TX cabinet installed as standards(If found)?
Does Battery cabinet installed as standards(If found)?
Are all the RRUs connected to the Stuitable CB and DCDU Fuse?
Are all the RRUs power cable diameter match with the RRU Power Standard?
Have LLVD and BLVD Parameters Checked?
Wrong installation
Is cabinets position of the site matched with the layout of the site ?
Is the ACDU installed in the site (in case there's no MDP in site)?
Does the Configuration match with the No of PSUs and No of batteries?
Have Tx Nodes installed as standards?
Bad installation
Are all Cabinets fixed to the I-beam by double nuts and have main earth?
Is AC cable for Power cabinet systemized and have lables and terminals?
Are All DCDU cables labled , heat Shrink and Systemized
Are All DC Cables and backup cables installed Without Loose?
Do all Radio units fixed and earthed?
Does all Radio units cables and fiber have (labels,colour code and systemization)?
Are All Cables Connected to TX Module have (Labels , Systmization and fabricated cables)
Does Batteries Installation checked ( Fixation , Covers , Gastube and Earth Cables )?
Does DC and AC Cover closed and Roxtic closed?
Are All cables In Tray Or ladder Systemized and tray closed?
Is Site Area clean From Unused Packages?
